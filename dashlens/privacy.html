Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

DashLens collects minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Camera Access: We access your camera to enable capturing images of car dashboard warning lights. Camera data is processed locally on your device and through our AI analysis service.
  • Photo Library: If you choose to select images from your gallery for analysis, we request access to your photo library.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve our app.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Analyze dashboard warning lights using AI technology
  • Provide accurate information about warning light meanings
  • Improve and optimize the app experience
  • Display relevant advertisements

3. AI Analysis

When you capture or select an image for analysis, the image is sent to our AI service (Claude by Anthropic) to identify and explain the warning lights. Images are processed for analysis purposes only and are not stored permanently.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

5. Data Storage

Analysis history and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal data or images on external servers beyond what is necessary for immediate processing.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Claude AI by Anthropic (Image Analysis)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Firebase Analytics (Usage Analytics)

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
