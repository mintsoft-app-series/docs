Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I scan a warning light?

A: Tap the camera button on the home screen, point your camera at the dashboard warning light, and take a photo. The AI will analyze the image and provide information about the warning light.

Q: Why isn't the camera working?

A: Make sure you've granted camera permissions to the app. Go to Settings > DashLens > Camera and enable access.

Q: How accurate is the AI analysis?

A: Our AI provides helpful information about common warning lights, but it should not replace professional diagnosis. For serious warnings, always consult a qualified mechanic.

Q: Can I analyze images from my photo gallery?

A: Yes! Tap the gallery icon to select an existing image of a dashboard warning light for analysis.

Q: How do I remove ads?

A: Currently, ads help us keep the app free. We may offer a premium ad-free version in the future.

Q: The app is crashing. What should I do?

A: Try these steps:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Restart your device
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store or Google Play
  • If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and OS version

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes. Images are only used for analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers. Your analysis history is stored locally on your device.
