Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why isn't my camera showing colors?

A: Make sure you've granted camera permissions to the app. Go to Settings > ColorSnapr > Camera and enable access.

Q: How do I copy a color code?

A: Simply tap on the color code (HEX, RGB, etc.) displayed on the screen to copy it to your clipboard.

Q: Can I save my favorite colors?

A: Yes! Tap the save button to add colors to your palette. You can access saved colors from the palette section.

Q: How do I remove ads?

A: Currently, ads help us keep the app free. We may offer a premium ad-free version in the future.

Q: The app is crashing. What should I do?

A: Try these steps:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Restart your device
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version
