Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I scan a QR code?

  1. Open the app and point your camera at any QR code or barcode
  2. The app will automatically detect and scan the code
  3. The scanned information will be displayed

What types of QR codes can I scan?

QR Snapr supports various QR code and barcode formats including:

  • QR Code
  • EAN-13, EAN-8
  • UPC-A, UPC-E
  • Code 39, Code 93, Code 128
  • ITF
  • Codabar
  • Data Matrix
  • PDF417
  • Aztec

How do I generate a QR code?

  1. Go to the "Generate" tab
  2. Select the type of QR code you want to create (URL, Text, Contact, etc.)
  3. Enter your information
  4. Tap "Generate" to create your QR code
  5. Save or share your generated QR code

How do I view my scan history?

Go to the "History" tab to see all your previously scanned codes. You can tap on any item to view details or perform actions.

How do I copy scanned content?

After scanning a QR code, tap the copy button to copy the content to your clipboard. You can also enable auto-copy in Settings.

How do I delete scan history?

Go to the "History" tab, swipe left on the item you want to delete, and tap "Delete".

Does the app work offline?

Yes! QR code scanning and generation works completely offline. Only ads require an internet connection.

Troubleshooting

Camera not working

  1. Go to your device Settings > QR Snapr > Camera
  2. Make sure camera permission is enabled
  3. Restart the app

QR code not scanning

  • Ensure adequate lighting
  • Hold the camera steady
  • Make sure the QR code is not damaged or blurry
  • Try adjusting the distance from the QR code

App is slow or crashing

  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Make sure you have the latest version installed
  3. Clear app cache in your device settings
  4. Restart your device
