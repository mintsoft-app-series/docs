Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2024

Introduction

QR Snapr ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our QR code scanner and generator application.

Information We Collect

Camera Access

  • We request camera permission solely to scan QR codes
  • Camera data is processed locally on your device
  • We do not record, store, or transmit any camera footage to external servers

Local Storage

  • Scan history and generated QR codes are stored locally on your device
  • This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the application

Third-Party Services

This app may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about how Google handles data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
