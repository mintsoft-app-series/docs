Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

FontSnapr ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Images: Photos you take or select for font identification. These images are processed for analysis but are not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including font analysis history stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app optimization and crash reporting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered font identification services
  • Generate font recommendations based on your analysis
  • Improve our app's performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your font analysis history is stored locally on your device. Images submitted for analysis are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Google Fonts API: To provide font recommendations
  • AI Processing Services: For font recognition analysis
  • Analytics Services: To improve app performance

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your local font analysis history at any time
  • Opt out of analytics data collection
  • Request information about data we hold about you

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
