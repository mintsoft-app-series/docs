Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does FontSnapr identify fonts?

A: FontSnapr uses advanced AI technology to analyze typography characteristics in your images, including letterforms, spacing, and style features to identify and match fonts.

Q: What image formats are supported?

A: FontSnapr supports common image formats including JPG, PNG, and HEIC. For best results, use clear, well-lit images with readable text.

Q: How accurate is the font identification?

A: Our AI provides highly accurate results for common fonts. Accuracy may vary for decorative, handwritten, or custom fonts. We provide multiple suggestions to help you find the best match.

Q: Can I use FontSnapr offline?

A: Font identification requires an internet connection for AI processing. However, you can view your saved analysis history offline.

Q: What languages are supported?

A: FontSnapr supports font identification for multiple languages including Korean, English, Japanese, and Chinese. The app interface is available in 18 languages.

Q: How do I delete my analysis history?

A: Go to the History tab in the app, swipe left on any item to delete it, or use the clear all option in settings to remove your entire history.

Q: Are the recommended fonts free to use?

A: Google Fonts recommendations are free for personal and commercial use. Korean font recommendations include licensing information. Always check the specific font license before use.

Tips for Best Results

  • Clear Images: Use high-quality, well-focused images
  • Good Lighting: Ensure text is well-lit without glare
  • Straight Angle: Capture text straight-on when possible
  • Sufficient Text: Include multiple characters for better accuracy
  • Clean Background: Simple backgrounds help improve recognition
